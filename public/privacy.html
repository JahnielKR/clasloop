Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 May 2026

Clasloop ("Clasloop", "we", "us") is a classroom tool that helps teachers run warmups, exit tickets and live quizzes, and remember what each class needs to review. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. It applies to the Clasloop web app and the Clasloop Android app.

Who uses Clasloop

Clasloop is designed for teachers and their students. Teachers create classes and may add students to a roster. Some students are minors. We do not knowingly collect more personal information than is needed to run a class. See Children's privacy below.

What we collect

  • Account information. When you sign up with email or with Google, we store your email address, display name, and (if provided) your avatar choice or Google profile photo. We do not receive your Google password.
  • Class & learning content. Classes, units, decks, questions, and the answers students submit during quizzes — used to score quizzes and to compute what a class should review next.
  • Scanned answer sheets (camera). If a teacher uses the optional sheet scanner, the Android app uses the device camera to photograph a printed answer sheet and read the marked bubbles on-device. See Camera & scanning.
  • Usage & diagnostics. Basic product-usage events and crash/error reports, used to keep the app working and to fix problems.

How we use your information

  • To provide the core service: create and run classes, quizzes and decks, grade responses, and surface spaced-repetition review suggestions.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To generate quiz questions from material a teacher provides (AI generation runs on teacher-entered content, not on student personal data).
  • To diagnose crashes and improve reliability and usability.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

Camera & scanning

The camera is used only when a teacher actively starts a scan. Bubble detection runs on the device. A scan image may be stored in a private storage bucket so the teacher can review the result; scan images are kept in a private location and auto-deleted after 7 days. The camera is never used in the background and no audio is recorded.

Where your data is stored (processors)

We use a small number of service providers to run Clasloop. They process data on our behalf under their own security and privacy terms:

  • Supabase — database, authentication and file storage.
  • Vercel — application hosting.
  • Google — optional "Sign in with Google".
  • Anthropic — AI generation of quiz questions from teacher-provided material.
  • PostHog — product-usage analytics.
  • Sentry — crash and error reporting.

Children's privacy

Clasloop is intended to be administered by a teacher or school. Where students are minors, the teacher or school is responsible for obtaining any consent required by local law (for example COPPA in the United States, or GDPR-K in the EU) before adding students. We collect from students only what is needed to run quizzes and track class progress, and we do not knowingly use student data for advertising or profiling. If you believe a child's data was provided without proper authorization, contact us at [SUPPORT EMAIL] and we will delete it.

Data retention & deletion

  • Account and class data are kept while your account is active.
  • You can delete your account from Settings → Delete account in the app. Deleting a teacher account removes the classes, decks, sessions and responses it owns.
  • Scan images auto-delete after 7 days.
  • To request deletion or a copy of your data, contact [SUPPORT EMAIL].

Your choices

  • Access, correct or delete your data (in-app, or by contacting us).
  • Use email sign-up instead of Google if you prefer.
  • Decline camera access — the scanner is optional; the rest of the app works without it.

Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored with access controls (row-level security) so that teachers see only their own classes and students see only the classes they belong to.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
